Karan Aujla Punjabi Singer
Karan Aujla (born 18 January 1997) is an Indian singer, lyricist, and rapper known for his work in Punjabi music. He is best known for the songs "Don't Look", "Chitta Kurta", "Don't Worry", "Hint", and "Sheikh". Ten of his singles as a lead artist have featured in UK Asian chart by Official Charts Company, while four have featured in Global YouTube music chart.
